About Founder

Mrs. Sangeeta Rajendra Bhagwat is a dedicated social worker passionate about helping senior citizens. Recognizing their unique needs, especially those who are bedridden or ill, she established Adharwad to provide specialized care. Her commitment to improving the lives of the elderly drives her to create a nurturing and supportive environment for all residents.
